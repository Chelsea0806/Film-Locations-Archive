St Paul's Cathedral is an Anglican cathedral in London. As the seat of the Bishop of London, the cathedral serves as the mother church of the Diocese of London.

Additionally, the cathedral is one of the most famous and most recognisable sights of London [1].

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004) shows the Geometric Staircase in the southwest bell tower, representing the staircase towards the Divination classroom.
