The Natural History Museum in London is a natural history museum with a large collection of specimens from all aspects of natural history. The Museum's stunning architecture has made it a popular choice as a filming location for everything from blockbuster movies to TV dramas, for example, Paddington, Tomb Raider.

Paddington is a 2014 live-action animated comedy film written and directed by Paul King, which is based on the stories of the character Paddington Bear created by Michael Bond. In Paddington, Millicent Clyde, who works as a taxidermist, shows Paddington her evil plans for him.

Tomb Raider is a 2018 action-adventure film directed by Roar Uthaug, with a screenplay by Geneva Robertson-Dworet and Alastair Siddons, from a story by Evan Daugherty and Robertson-Dworet. In Tomb Raider III, Lara visits an Egyptology exhibit in the Natural History Museum in London.
