Leadenhall Market is a covered market in London, located on Gracechurch Street. It is one of the oldest markets in London, dating from the 14th century, and is located in the historic center of the City of London financial district [1].

Leadenhall Market is one of the main locations for Diagon Alley in the Harry Potter film series.

In 《Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone》:

00:20:30: Hagrid takes Harry past Leadenhall Market and from the Leaky Cauldron into Diagon Alley to buy Harry's school supplies: wands, owls, etc.

Address: Gracechurch St, London EC3V 1LT, UK
