The Millennium Bridge in London is about 325 meters long and the design concept is "Blade of light". The structure of the London Millennium Bridge is bright, with steel cables connecting various structural components to support the bridge body, and the steel cables are not directly connected to the bridge body. This unique suspension bridge design comes from the handwriting of British architect Norman Foster [3].
